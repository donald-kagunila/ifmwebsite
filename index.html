Academic Campuses

Main Campus

IFM Main campus was set up in 1972. It stands as the oldest higher learning financial institution in Tanzania, located in Dar es Salaam City Centre, Shaaban Robert Street, opposite the National Museum.

Learn More >>

Mwanza Campus

IFM Mwanza Teaching Centre (IFM-MCT) was established in 2013. The Campus is located in Mwanza City, Isamilo area, Machemba Road. It is housed in the compound of UWT, opposite Sekou Toure Hospital-a walking distance from City Centre.
